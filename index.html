What “swim team lane capacity pressure” means:
Swim team practices reserve a number of lap lanes for a scheduled block. Those swimmers are not counted the same as open swim visitors because they mainly affect lane availability, deck supervision, and lap-swim capacity. AquaIQ uses the swimmers and reserved lanes to estimate whether normal lap/open swim access will be constrained.
